The inventory of the Louis Niedermeyer (1802-1861) collection, preserved by the Niedermeyer Foundation in Nyon, is a new cataloging project started on 13 July 2015.

Die Katalogisierung des Nachlasses von Louis Niedermeyer (1802-1861), welcher in der Stiftung Niedermeyer in Nyon aufbewahrt wird, ist ein neues Katalogisierungsprojekt, das am 13. Juli 2015 in Angriff genommen wurde.

Le catalogage du fonds Louis Niedermeyer (1802-1861) réuni par l’Association Niedermeyer à Nyon est un nouveau projet de catalogage qui vient de débuter le 13 juillet 2015.

L'inventariazione del fondo Louis Niedermeyer (1802-1861), conservato dall'Associazione Niedermeyer a Nyon, è un nuovo progetto di catalogazione iniziato il 13 luglio 2015.

The collection, comprising nearly the whole output of Niedermeyer transmitted as manuscripts and printed editions, is a particularly interesting source of information with regard to the transmission of music and the practice of music publishing in the 19th century.

See the project page for more information about the project in Nyon.
