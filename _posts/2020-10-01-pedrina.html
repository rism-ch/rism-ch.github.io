The ensemble "La Pedrina", conducted by Francesco Saverio Pedrini, will once again perform in two concerts with the programme from the last CD produced by RISM Switzerland (http://rism-ch.org/cd-marenzio.html): "Il Pastor fido dei Grigioni", with madrigals by Luca Marenzio from a source preserved in Zuoz. The concerts will take place on 24 October in Bellinzona (as part of the "Cantar di pietre" (https://www.cantardipietre.ch/) concert series) and on 25 October in Zuoz.

Das Ensemble „La Pedrina“ unter der Leitung von Francesco Saverio Pedrini tritt wieder in zwei Konzerte mit dem Programm aus der letzten von RISM Schweiz realisierten CD (http://rism-ch.org/cd-marenzio.html) auf: „Il Pastor fido dei Grigioni“ mit Madrigalen von Luca Marenzio aus einer in Zuoz aufbewahrten Quelle. Die Konzerte finden am 24. Oktober in Bellinzona (als Teil der Konzertreihe „Cantar di pietre“ (https://www.cantardipietre.ch/)) und am 25. Oktober in Zuoz statt.

L'ensemble "La Pedrina", dirigé par Francesco Saverio Pedrini, donnera à nouveau deux concerts avec le programme du dernier CD réalisé par le RISM Suisse (http://rism-ch.org/cd-marenzio.html) : "Il Pastor fido dei Grigioni", avec des madrigaux de Luca Marenzio provenant d'une source conservée à Zuoz. Les concerts auront lieu le 24 octobre à Bellinzona (dans le cadre de la série de concerts "Cantar di pietre" (https://www.cantardipietre.ch/)) et le 25 octobre à Zuoz.

L'ensemble "La Pedrina", diretto da Francesco Saverio Pedrini, si esibirà nuovamente in due concerti con il programma dell'ultimo CD realizzato da RISM Svizzera (http://rism-ch.org/cd-marenzio.html): "Il Pastor fido dei Grigioni", con madrigali di Luca Marenzio da una fonte conservata a Zuoz. I concerti si terranno il 24 ottobre a Bellinzona (nell'ambito della rassegna "Cantar di pietre" (https://www.cantardipietre.ch/) e il 25 ottobre a Zuoz.

The CD recording was released in 2017 for the label Claves Records (https://www.claves.ch/). The programme was performed in Zurich and Zuoz in 2017. After three years there is again the possibility to listen to this music on Sunday, 25 October at the place where it is kept, in the church of San Luzi. The concert, which puts a rare musical treasure from the Upper Engadine in the spotlight, is organised by the municipality of Zuoz.
