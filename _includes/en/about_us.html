About us

Since its foundation in 1956 the Swiss RISM branch has recorded systematically more than 10'000 prints before 1800, collective prints of the 16th, 17th and 18th centuries and other works about music, more than 34'000 manuscripts after 1600 as well as more than 21'000 prints of the 19th and 20th centuries.

Since 1996 the Swiss RISM branch is organized as an independent association mainly financed through contributions for projects and commissions for inventorization by libraries and archives. The following founding members established Swiss RISM Association on June 21st 1996:

  • Swiss Musicological Society (SMG)
  • Association of Swiss Music Collections (ASCM)
  • Swiss Society for the Rights of Authors of Musical Works (SUISA)
  • Swiss Music Council (SMR: german version, french version)
