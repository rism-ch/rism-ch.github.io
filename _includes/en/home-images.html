Several infrastructure projects are centred on the music sources. In many of them, digitization plays a major role.

The cataloging software Muscat is co-developed by the Swiss RISM. It is based on international standards, and tailored to the requirements of a music catalog.

The core work is cataloguing music sources. A representative manuscript is the autograph of Alberik Zwyssig's Swiss Psalm.

The Swiss RISM supports many Swiss archives and libraries in ordering, preserving and compiling inventories of their music collections.
